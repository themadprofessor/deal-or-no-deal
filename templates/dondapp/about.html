About Us

This website was designed to make it easier for students to search for deals. Our idea was to allow users to sign up to our webiste and be able to view or post deals.

Users are able to either search for the type of deal they are looking for using the search bar or by using the category filters.

We also implemented a feature on the homepage to allow users to view the 'best', 'top' and 'new' deals on the website.
